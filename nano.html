NaNoWriMo

This page is not directly C64-related, but contains selected National Novel Writing Month novels written by Cadaver.

The Agents of Metal trilogy is a reinterpretation / reboot of the story from the Metal Warrior games, primarily 1 & 4.

Story of the game Hessian is based on parts of Kyräilevien Ohjelmoijien Kerho - The Revenge.

The Finnish language novel Järjestelmän Petturi (System Traitor) is based on a game concept from 1991, which was started on Amiga but abandoned without getting very far.

These novels may contain immature, violent and offensive material, so read at own risk.
